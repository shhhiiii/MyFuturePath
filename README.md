# 🛤️ My Future Path — Career Guidance Assistant Bot

> Hello! **My Future Path** will help you find an interesting profession or a direction for your development. You can use it anytime.

---

## 🚀 How to Start

1. Find the bot on Telegram.
2. Press the **Start** button or enter the `/start` command.
3. You will see the main menu.

---

## 📌 Main Menu

* **Find a Profession** — Tell us a little about yourself (what you like, your strengths), and the bot will select suitable career directions for you.
* **My Interests** — Your saved interests and chosen career paths will be stored here.
* **Settings** — Change the communication style or language.

---

## 🔍 How the Profession Matcher Works

1. Click **"Find a Profession"**.
2. Write a couple of sentences: what your hobbies are, and what you enjoy doing.
3. The bot will analyze your text and provide recommendations:
   * Similar professions.
   * Skills you can develop.
   * Possible directions for your future career choice.

---

## 🎯 My Interests

This section will display the interests you entered when creating your profile (you will be able to edit them in future updates).

---

## 🧠 Artificial Intelligence Integration

While the bot has pre-set responses, it also features an **AI Mode**. 
In this mode, the responses become more flexible, intuitive, and closer to a real-time consultation with a career specialist.

---

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Do I need to pay to use the bot?**
> No, the bot is completely free.

**What if I didn't find a suitable profession?**
> Try describing yourself differently. For example, instead of saying *"I want to work in IT,"* try writing, *"I love figuring out how computers work and programming."*

---

## 🙌 Who is this bot for?

* **Teenagers** who are choosing their very first profession.
* **Professionals** who are tired of their current jobs and want to switch fields.
* **Anyone** looking for new paths and opportunities in life.
